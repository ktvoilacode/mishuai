FAQ

Questions, answered.

Which platforms do you specialise in?

Filevine, Clio and GoHighLevel — plus the automation layer (Zapier, Make) and modern AI models that tie them together. We go deep on these rather than spreading thin.

Do you replace our existing software?

Almost never. Most firms already have good tools — we make them work together intelligently and remove the manual work in between, so you get more from what you already pay for.

Do you work with both US and UK firms?

Yes. We serve law firms across the United States and United Kingdom, and build with each market's data-protection and regulatory requirements in mind.

Do you build custom AI tools?

Yes. Alongside platform integrations we build bespoke AI assistants and automations — document drafting, extraction, intake routing — designed around your firm's specific workflows.

How does an engagement start?

With a discovery call. We map your workflows, identify where time and revenue leak away, and come back with a practical plan and quick wins — no jargon, no overselling.

What size of firm do you work with?

From solo and boutique practices to growing multi-team firms. If you run on Filevine, Clio or GoHighLevel and feel the friction between them, we can help.
